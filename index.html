About

Dr. Bhaskar Bhattacharyya earned his PhD from Visva-Bharati University. His research lies at the intersection of health economics and development economics, combining theoretical modelling with rigorous empirical analysis using large-scale survey datasets. His work focuses on maternal and child health, social capital and health behavior, ageing and vulnerability in developing economies.
